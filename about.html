About Us

"Committed to safety, innovation, and custom solutions for your projects."

At Grun Tech Solutions Inc.

Our primary goal is to provide a safe and healthy work environment that promotes the well-being of our employees and all individuals affected by our business operations.

  • Ensuring the provision and maintenance of secure and healthy workplaces for our employees, contractors, and visitors.
  • Developing and upholding a health and risk management system in alignment with DOLE's DO 13.
  • Identifying potential hazards, conducting risk assessments, and implementing effective risk management and control strategies at our work sites.
  • Pursuing continual improvement in health and safety by establishing and accomplishing measurable health and safety objectives and targets, with a focus on reducing risks and preventing work-related injuries and illnesses.
  • Encouraging and supporting employees in actively contributing to the enhancement of health and safety through appropriate consultation, information sharing, support, and training.
